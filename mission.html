Our Mission

Founded in 2025, PowerOnMe LLC is developing next-generation fuel cell systems that convert organic green fuels, ranging from human metabolites to agricultural byproducts, into electricity for autonomous sensing, health regulation, and off-grid power applications. At the core of our innovation is a new class of mixed-reactant fuel cells (MRFCs), engineered through the integration of AI-driven catalyst design and microfluidic control, aiming for compact, low-cost, and high-energy-density power sources that outperform lithium batteries and overcome the limitations of traditional fuel cells.
